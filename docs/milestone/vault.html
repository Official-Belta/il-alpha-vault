Strategy

How the vault works

The EWMA volatility oracle tracks price variance on every swap. When variance exceeds the threshold, the keeper bot removes LP entirely. Re-enters automatically when volatility stabilizes.

ERC-4626 Standard
Standard vault interface. Instantly composable with DeFi protocols. Usable as collateral on Morpho, Euler, and others.
On-chain Vol Oracle
EWMA-based tick variance tracking. Zero off-chain dependencies. Every swap updates the oracle.
Automatic LP Protection
Full LP removal during high volatility. Not range adjustment — complete withdrawal. Zero IL exposure during risk periods.
Instant Withdrawal
No lockup. Withdraw anytime — even when vault is paused. TWAP check prevents sandwich attacks.
Volume Spike Trigger
Bypasses cooldown on abnormal volume for immediate LP removal. Extra safety net for sudden market movements.
